## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will learn two short patterns in English.
They help us say who we are.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

Two fixed patterns:
I am — starts a sentence about myself.
You are — starts a sentence about the person we're talking to.
Then we add a word like happy or sad.
Example:
I am happy — I am happy.
You are sad — You are sad.

---

I am happy.
