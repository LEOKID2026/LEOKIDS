## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will learn what the first sound in an English word is.
We hear a word — and find the sound that starts it.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

The word cat starts with a "k" sound.
The word dog starts with a "d" sound.
The word sun starts with an "s" sound.
We're not writing yet — just listening.

---

## 3. Example

In the word mom, we hear an "m" sound at the start.
The word mom starts with an "m" sound.
In the word pen, we hear a "p" sound at the start.
The word pen starts with a "p" sound.

---

## 4. Let's solve together

What is the first sound in cat?
Say it slowly: cat.
The word cat starts with a "k" sound.
