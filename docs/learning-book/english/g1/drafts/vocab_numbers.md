## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will learn numbers in English from 0 to 10.
Numbers help us count things and talk about how many.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

Every number has a name in English.
zero — 0
one — 1
two — 2
three — 3
four — 4
five — 5
six — 6
seven — 7
eight — 8
nine — 9
ten — 10
