## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will calculate volume of a rectangular prism directly — all three dimensions in one multiplication.
Volume = a × b × h — length × width × height.
(Different from "prism": here it's a box — all faces are rectangles, without separating a "base area" step.)

---

## 2. Simple explanation

:::geometry-diagram
type: box_basic
:::

Rectangular prism — 3 dimensions: a, b, h.
Volume = a × b × h
Order of multiplication doesn't matter: 5 × 4 × 6 = 4 × 6 × 5 = 6 × 5 × 4
Unit: cm³.

---

## 3. Example

Rectangular prism 5 cm × 4 cm × 6 cm:
- 5 × 4 = 20
- 20 × 6 = 120 cm³
Or: 5 × 4 × 6 = 120 — multiply all three together.
