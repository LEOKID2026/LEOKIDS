## 1. What are we learning?

Today we're going to learn to find the whole when we know the half.
Half = one part out of two equal parts — so the whole = two halves.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

If half of the whole is a certain number — the whole is twice that number.
If the half is 6, the whole is 12 — because 6 + 6 = 12.
How do you find it?
- Multiply by 2, or
- Add the half twice: 6 + 6
Important: this works only when the half is really a half — an equal part of two.

---

## 3. Visual / concrete example

Half of the whole is 6 — what is the whole?
6 + 6 = 12
If the half is 6 → the whole is 12
[ 6 ] | [ 6 ]
half   half
= 12 whole

## 4. Let's solve together

Half of the whole is 6. What is the whole?
Half = 6 — that's one part out of two equal parts.
The other half is also 6.
6 + 6 = 12
The whole is 12

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

Half of the whole is 5.
What is the whole?
Half = 5 — that's one part out of two equal parts.
The other half is also 5.
5 + 5 = 10
The whole is 10
