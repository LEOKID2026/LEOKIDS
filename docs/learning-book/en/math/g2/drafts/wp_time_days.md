## 1. What are we learning?

Today we're going to learn how many days pass from one day to another on the row of days.
Count hops — without counting the start day as the first step.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

How many days from day X to day Y?
→ Count hops after the start day, up to the target day.
Before you answer:
1. What do we know? — which day we start on and which day we reach
2. What are we asked? — how many days pass
3. What do we do? — count hops on the row
Important: don't count the start day. No clock or dates in the month.
[Sunday] [Monday] [Tuesday] [Wednesday] [Thursday] [Friday] [Saturday]

## 4. Let's solve together

From Sunday to Thursday — how many days pass?
Step 1 — What do we know?
Start: Sunday. Reach: Thursday.
Step 2 — What are we asked?
How many days pass.
Step 3 — What do we do?
After Sunday: (1) Monday → (2) Tuesday → (3) Wednesday → (4) Thursday

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

From Monday to Friday — how many days pass?
Step 1 — What do we know? Start on Monday, reach Friday.
Step 2 — What are we asked? How many days pass.
Step 3 — Count hops: after Monday: (1) Tuesday → (2) Wednesday → (3) Thursday → (4) Friday
4 days pass
