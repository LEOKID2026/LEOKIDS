## 1. What are we learning?

Today we're going to learn about pairs that make 10 — two numbers that together give 10.
When you know these pairs, adding and subtracting get easier!

---

## 2. Simple explanation

There are special pairs that always add up to 10:
1 + 9, 2 + 8, 3 + 7, 4 + 6, 5 + 5
Important: order doesn't matter!
3 + 7 = 10 and also 7 + 3 = 10

---

## 4. Let's solve together

8 + ? = 10 — what is the missing number?
In the ten-frame — fill 8 spots
2 spots are left empty
The partner of 8 is 2 — because 8 + 2 = 10
8 + 2 = 10

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

4 + ? = 10 — what is the missing number?
In the ten-frame — fill 4 spots.
6 spots are left empty.
The partner of 4 is 6 — because 4 + 6 = 10.
4 + 6 = 10
