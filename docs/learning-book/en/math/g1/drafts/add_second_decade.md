## 1. What are we learning?

Today we're going to learn to add numbers when the answer is in the teens.
The teens = the numbers right after 10: 11, 12, 13 … up to 19.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

The teens are all the numbers between 11 and 19.
They come after 10 and before 20.
A handy way: first make 10, then add what's left.
8 + 5 → first 8 + 2 = 10, then 10 + 3 = 13

## 4. Let's solve together

7 + 6 = ?
7 needs 3 to get to 10 (the partner of 7 is 3)
From 6, 3 are left (6 = 3 + 3)
7 + 3 = 10, then 10 + 3 = 13
7 + 6 = 13 — 13 is in the teens (between 11 and 19).

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

9 + 4 = ?
9 needs 1 to get to 10.
From 4, 3 are left (4 = 1 + 3)
9 + 1 = 10, then 10 + 3 = 13.
9 + 4 = 13
