## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will solve unit conversion: cm ↔ meters.
100 cm = 1 m

---

## 2. Simple explanation

350 cm = 350 ÷ 100 = 3.5 m (3 m and 50 cm)
2 m = 2 × 100 = 200 cm

---

## 3. Visual / concrete example

1,000 cm = 10 m

---

## 4. Let's solve together

350 cm = ? m
350 ÷ 100 = 3.5
3.5 m.

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

850 cm = ? m
100 cm = 1 m → cm→m = ÷100.
850 ÷ 100 = 8.5
Check: 8.5 × 100 = 850
The answer: 8.5 m
