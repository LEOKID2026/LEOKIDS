## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will learn to find a ratio from two quantities.
12 pens and 8 erasers.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

Ratio pens:erasers = 12:8
Simplify by 4: 12/4 : 8/4 = 3:2

---

## 3. Visual / concrete example

15:10 = 3:2

---

## 4. Let's solve together

12 pens, 8 erasers — ratio pens:erasers?
12:8.
÷4 → 3:2.
3:2.

---

## 5. Try it yourself

---

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

18 cards and 12 stickers — ratio cards:stickers
Write: 18:12.
Simplify by 6: 18/6 : 12/6 = 3:2.
Check: cannot simplify further — 3 and 2 share no common factor except 1
The answer: 3:2
