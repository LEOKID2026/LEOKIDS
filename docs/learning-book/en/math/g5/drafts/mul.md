## 1. What are we learning?

Today we'll learn multiplication — including two-digit multiplication and breaking-apart strategies.
Multiplication helps with quick math and solving problems.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

Multiplication strategies:
Multiplying by friendly numbers: 25 × 4 = 100
Breaking apart: 25 × 36 = 25 × 30 + 25 × 6 = 750 + 150 = 900
Order doesn't matter: 36 × 25 = 25 × 36
25 × 36:
25 × 30 = 750
25 × 6 = 150
750 + 150 = 900

---

## 3. Visual / concrete example

48 × 25:
48 × 25 = 48 × 100 ÷ 4 = 4,800 ÷ 4 = 1,200
(or: 50 × 25 − 2 × 25 = 1,250 − 50 = 1,200)

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

Work out: 24 × 35 = ?
Break apart: 30 + 5 = 35
24 × 30 = 720
24 × 5 = 120
720 + 120 = 840
24 × 35 = 840
