## 1. What are we learning?

Today we'll learn to solve an addition equation with a blank — __.
We find the missing number.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

3,250 + __ = 8,400
Ask: what do you add to 3,250 to reach 8,400?
__ = 8,400 − 3,250 = 5,150
Check: 3,250 + 5,150 = 8,400

---

## 3. Visual / concrete example

__ + 4,200 = 9,500
__ = 9,500 − 4,200 = 5,300

---

## 4. Let's solve together

3,250 + __ = 8,400 — what is missing?
__ = 8,400 − 3,250
__ = 5,150
5,150.

## 5. Try it yourself

__ + 5,300 = 12,700 — what is the missing number?
Try to solve it on your own.
On the next page we'll check the steps and the answer together.

---

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

__ + 5,300 = 12,700 — what is the missing number?
__ = 12,700 − 5,300
__ = 7,400
Check: 7,400 + 5,300 = 12,700
__ = 7,400
