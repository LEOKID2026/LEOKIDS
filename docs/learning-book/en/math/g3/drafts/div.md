## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will strengthen division — equal sharing — with bigger numbers than we learned in Grade 2.
When the division comes out exactly — there is no remainder.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

84 ÷ 4 — 84 balls into 4 equal groups:
How many in each group? 4 × 21 = 84 → 84 ÷ 4 = 21
96 ÷ 3: 3 × 32 = 96 → 96 ÷ 3 = 32
Check: divisor × quotient = dividend — always check with multiplication.
Remainder? On this page only when it comes out even — remainder is on the division with remainder page.

## 4. Let's solve together

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

Calculate: 75 ÷ 5
Ask: 5 × ? = 75
5 × 10 = 50, 5 × 15 = 75 → the quotient is 15
Check: 5 × 15 = 75
75 ÷ 5 = 15
