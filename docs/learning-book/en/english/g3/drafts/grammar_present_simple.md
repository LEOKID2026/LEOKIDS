## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will learn the Present Simple tense.
Positive, negative, and question forms.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

Positive:
I play tennis.

Negative:
I do not play tennis.

Question:
Do you play tennis?

---

## 3. Example

A child playing tennis.

I play tennis.

I play tennis.

---

## 4. Let's solve together

Question: how do we say "I play tennis"?

I play tennis

Answer: I play tennis

---

## 5. Try it yourself

A girl playing tennis.

She plays tennis.

Try to solve it yourself.
On the next page, we'll check the way and the answer together.

---

## 6. Let's check together

Exercise: a girl playing tennis.

The solution:

She plays tennis.

She plays tennis.

Well done! After she, he, or it, we add s to the verb — plays. After I, we don't add s — I play.
