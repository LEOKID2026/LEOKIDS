## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will solve a subtraction equation with a missing number — for example __ − 18 = 42.
We need to find the bigger number (the one we subtract from).

---

## 2. Simple explanation

__ − 18 = 42
Ask: from some number we subtracted 18 and got 42 — what was the number?
42 + 18 = 60
60 − 18 = 42
Rule: when the blank is before the minus sign — add the other two numbers.

---

## 3. Visual / concrete example

__ − 25 = 37
37 + 25 = 62
62 − 25 = 37

---

## 4. Let's solve together

__ − 14 = 56 — what is the missing number?
56 + 14 = 70
70 − 14 = 56
The missing number is 70.

---

## 5. Try it yourself

__ − 34 = 48 — what is the missing number?
Try to solve it on your own.
On the next page we'll check the steps and the answer together.

---

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

__ − 34 = 48 — what is the missing number?
From some number we subtracted 34 and got 48 — what was the number?
48 + 34 = 82
Check: 82 − 34 = 48
The missing number is 82

## 7. Let's practice!

Now you know how to solve a subtraction equation with a missing number.
In practice you'll find __ before the minus sign.
