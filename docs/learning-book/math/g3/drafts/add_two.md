## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will learn to add two numbers up to 1,000.
We break them into hundreds, tens, and ones — and remember to regroup when the ones or tens add up to 10 or more.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

Steps for adding in your head:
Break each number apart:
hundreds + tens + ones
Add the ones — if you get 10 or more, carry 1 to the tens
Add the tens (including the carry) — if you get 10 or more, carry 1 to the hundreds
Add the hundreds
248 + 156:
- ones: 8 + 6 = 14 → write 4, carry 1
- tens: 4 + 5 + 1 = 10 → write 0, carry 1
- hundreds: 2 + 1 + 1 = 4
- answer: 404

---

## 3. Visual / concrete example

375 + 248:
300 + 70 + 5 = 375
200 + 40 + 8 = 248
ones: 5 + 8 = 13 → 3, carry 1
tens: 7 + 4 + 1 = 12 → 2, carry 1
hundreds: 3 + 2 + 1 = 6
375 + 248 = 623

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

Calculate: 519 + 274
ones: 9 + 4 = 13 → write 3, carry 1
tens: 1 + 7 + 1 = 9
hundreds: 5 + 2 = 7
519 + 274 = 793
