## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will strengthen the times tables — multiplying numbers 1 through 10.
Multiplication = adding equal groups again and again.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

6 × 7 = 6 times 7 = 7 + 7 + 7 + 7 + 7 + 7 = 42
7 × 8 = 7 groups of 8 = 56
Tip: 7 × 8 = 8 × 7 — same answer (order doesn't matter).
Link to Grade 3: strong tables help with multiplying by tens and with word problems.

---

## 3. Visual / concrete example

9 × 4 — 9 groups, 4 in each:
4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 = 36
9 × 4 = 36

---

## 4. Let's solve together

Calculate: 8 × 6 = ?
8 × 6 = 6 × 8 (easier to think: 6 groups of 8).
8 + 8 + 8 + 8 + 8 + 8 = 48
8 × 6 = 48

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

Calculate: 9 × 6
9 × 6 = 6 × 9 (order doesn't matter)
6 + 6 + 6 + 6 + 6 + 6 + 6 + 6 + 6 = 54
9 × 6 = 54
