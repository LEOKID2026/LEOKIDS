## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will learn percent of a quantity.
40% of 250.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

40% = 40/100 = 0.4
40% of 250 = 0.4 × 250 = 100
Or: 250 × 40 ÷ 100 = 100

---

## 3. Visual / concrete example

25% of 80 = 20

---

## 4. Let's solve together

40% of 250 = ?
250 × 40 = 10,000
10,000 ÷ 100 = 100
100.

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

Calculate: 30% of 150
150 × 30 = 4,500
4,500 ÷ 100 = 45
Check: 45 is 30 parts out of 100 of 150
The answer: 45
