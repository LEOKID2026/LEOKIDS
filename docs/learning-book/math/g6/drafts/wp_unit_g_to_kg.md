## 1. What are we learning?

Today we will solve unit conversion: grams ↔ kilograms.
1,000 g = 1 kg

---

## 2. Simple explanation

2,500 g = 2,500 ÷ 1,000 = 2.5 kg
3 kg = 3 × 1,000 = 3,000 g

---

## 3. Visual / concrete example

500 g = 0.5 kg

---

## 4. Let's solve together

2,500 g = ? kg

2.5 kg.

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

4,500 g = ? kg
1,000 g = 1 kg → g→kg = ÷1,000.
4,500 ÷ 1,000 = 4.5
Check: 4.5 × 1,000 = 4,500
The answer: 4.5 kg
