## 1. What are we learning?

Today we'll learn to solve a division equation — find the missing divisor.
2,400 ÷ __ = 60

---

## 2. Simple explanation

2,400 ÷ __ = 60
__ = 2,400 ÷ 60 = 40
Check: 2,400 ÷ 40 = 60
Rule:
Missing divisor = dividend ÷ quotient

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

4,500 ÷ __ = 75 — what is the missing divisor?
Missing divisor = dividend ÷ quotient
__ = 4,500 ÷ 75 = 60
Check: 4,500 ÷ 60 = 75
__ = 60
