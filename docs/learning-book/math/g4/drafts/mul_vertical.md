**Content scope:** Vertical multiplication — two-digit number × one digit. Regrouping (carrying). No two two-digit numbers.

---

## 1. What are we learning?

Today we'll learn vertical multiplication — a two-digit number times one digit.
Write the numbers one above the other, multiply from right to left, and remember to carry.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

23 × 4 — steps:
Write 23 on top, 4 on the bottom (lined up on the right)
Ones: 3 × 4 = 12 → write 2, carry 1
Tens: 2 × 4 = 8, + 1 carry = 9
92
34 × 5:
- ones: 4 × 5 = 20 → 0, carry 2
- tens: 3 × 5 = 15, + 2 = 17
- answer: 170

## 6. Common mistake — watch out!

Work out: 37 × 6 = ?
Ones: 7 × 6 = 42 → write 2, carry 4.
Tens: 3 × 6 = 18, + 4 carry = 22.
Write 22 → answer: 222.
37 × 6 = 222
