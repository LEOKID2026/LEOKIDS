## 1. What are we learning?

Today in geometry we will learn the perimeter of a triangle — the sum of all three sides.
We will start with small sides: 3, 4, and 5 cm.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

:::geometry-diagram
type: triangle_perimeter
:::

A triangle has only 3 sides — not 4!
Perimeter = side1 + side2 + side3
Go around the triangle — add each side once.

---

## 3. Example

A triangle with sides 3, 4, and 5 cm:
3 + 4 = 7
7 + 5 = 12
Perimeter: 12 cm.

---

## 4. Let's solve together
