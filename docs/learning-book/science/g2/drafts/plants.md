## 1. What are we learning?

Today in science we will learn how a plant grows from a seed — and what happens when water is missing.

---

## 2. Simple explanation

A plant's growth cycle:
Seed — small, found in fruit or in a packet
Germination — the seed opens, a root and stem come out
Plant — green leaves, the plant grows
A plant needs sun, water, and soil.
Without water — the seed does not sprout or the plant wilts.

---

## 3. Example

A child plants seeds in a pot in the classroom.
After a few days — a small root, a green leaf.
The plant grows slowly.
If you forget to pour water — the plant does not grow well.

---

## 4. Let's solve together

What happens to a seed without water?
A seed needs water to sprout.
Without water — the seed does not sprout, and therefore does not grow.
It does not sprout and does not grow.

---

## 5. Try it yourself

A plant grows in a bright window.
You move it to a dark room for a week.
What will happen to its leaves?
Try to solve it on your own.
On the next page we will check the steps and the answer together.

## 6. Let's check together

The question was:
A plant moved to a dark room — what will happen to its leaves?
Plants make food for themselves using sunlight.
Without light — the plant cannot make food, the leaves start to turn yellow and fall off.
The answer: the leaves will turn yellow and the plant will weaken.
